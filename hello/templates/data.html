Why linked data?

Linked data is a method for structuring and connecting information on the web in a way that makes it more meaningful and useful. It's based on a set of principles and technologies that enable data to be linked, shared, and queried across different sources and applications.

Linked data is employed in this project to integrate and enrich geo-annotated event data related to resilience in war situations. This approach facilitates data integration, semantic enrichment, and advanced querying, enabling a more comprehensive and insightful analysis of the conflict's impact on public facilities and regions.
